What is CDD?

CDD is the most effect way to reduce the average cost of your software

CDD analyses how various aspects of software development have an influence the total cost of a software developement. This enables it to pin point cost increasing practices in ongoing projects, optimize new projects and spread best practices. Software engineering is complex. Denying this fact leads to nothing good. Understanding the relationship between various software aspects will take some effort. On the positive side, you will have learned something that will serve you for the rest of your career. CDD is knowledge that is offered for free by the best engineers in the work.
